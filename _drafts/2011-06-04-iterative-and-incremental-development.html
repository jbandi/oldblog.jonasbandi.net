Kevlin Henney gives an interesting non-software related example of incremental development in his presentation Putting the "re" into Architecture.

A skyscraper built in Honkong: While the upper stores are still under construction, the lower stories are completely finished. People are living and working in the lower stories. They are paying for the building before it is actually finished: A gradual release system!
